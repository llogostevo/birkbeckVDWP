What is Silicon Hack?

Silicon Hack is part of the Level 3 sixth form offering at the computing department in Central Foundation Boys School, Islington.

Our aim is to provide students a route into the tech industry

Students will achieve qualifications to provide progression into the workplace or university

We offer a vocational pathway where students learn both the technical and soft skills required to move onto a pathway in the IT sector

Students work on projects throughout the year to get an understanding of how the real world of tech works
