Who Are We?

An offshoot of the Computing Department @ Central Foundation Boys School. Located next door to the world famous Silicon Roundabout Central Foundation Boys' School aims to provide:

...a world class curriculum for the students: whilst based around traditional subjects it must reflect the world as it is today and prepare the students for the future

This part of the mission statement led to the formation of Silicon Hack. Working under the umbrella of Central Foundation Boys' Schools sixth form provision we have developed a course that moves away from the traditional classroom based approach.

Here you will learn technical skills that include:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Intro to JavaScript
  • Intro to Python

More importantly you will learn the soft skills required to progress either into University, an Apprenticeship or the workplace. Skills such as:

  • Teamwork
  • Confidence
  • Integrity
  • Leadership
  • Independence
