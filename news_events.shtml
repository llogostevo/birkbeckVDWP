Each month we have an activity at Silicon Hack. Our students get some great experiences, as you will see below. These experiences are all part of the learning process at Silicon Hack. Whether that be getting first hand advice from industry experts or seeing how a real life tech company works each activity is designed to give our students an understanding of the world of tech.

Salesforce Trip

Use the Salesforce

"Right up at the top of heron tower, with fantastic views of all of the city. We learnt about cloud computing, the way teams were made up in salesforce and of course we had a fantastic lunch spread out for us with as much chocolate as we could eat."

A Student

Google Trip

Hey Google!

"Need I say more?!? An amazing trip with some exciting talks and insights as to how the world of Google works. We learnt about how they are moving into AI with deep mind. We also had a go on the famous google slide ☺ "

A Student

Curve Trip

Ahead of the Curve

"We learnt about their new App and how they are slowly changing the dynamic of payment through card. An exciting day that got us seeing what it really is like to be in a startup."

A Student

Inspire Mentoring

Mentors in March

"We welcomed mentors from Google, Curve, Salesforce and several freelancers. They worked with us on job opportunities and prospects of getting into the tech sector. It was great to meet people form the real world of tech and learn about what they do in their day to day jobs"

A Student

NLA Trip

Build it Up

Our trip to NLA really was hands on. We worked with experts designing 3d models using Google Sketch up and were then able to visualise our model within the London landscape with virtual reality software. It was amazing to see my building next to the Shard.

A Student
